Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Go to the Master Data section in your Caddayn Biller dashboard.
  2. Click on Items.
  3. Click Create to add a new item.
  4. In the item creation form, select the appropriate Brand from the dropdown.
  5. Enter the product name using a logical naming convention (e.g., "ABC Shoes - Size 8 - Red").
  6. Fill in other details such as SKU, price, and stock information.
  7. Repeat for each variant you need to manage.

Conclusion

By creating separate product entries for each variant, you can easily manage, track, and report on all your products, no matter how many subdivisions they have. Caddayn Biller provides the flexibility and tools you need for efficient product management.
