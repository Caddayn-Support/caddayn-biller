How to Download Tax Invoices for Monthly Submission

Can I take monthly tax invoices from the software?

Yes, you can easily generate and download tax invoices directly from Caddayn Biller.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Ensure GST Configuration:
    • Go to Settings → Company Profile and verify your GSTIN is correctly entered.
    • Check that your invoice template includes all required fields: GSTIN, HSN/SAC codes, invoice number, date, buyer/seller details, and tax breakdown.
  2. Create and Customize Invoices:
    • When creating invoices, fill in all product/service details, including applicable GST rates and HSN/SAC codes.
    • Review the tax columns to ensure correct tax calculation and breakdown.
  3. Generate Monthly Invoice Reports:
    • Navigate to the Reports section in the main menu.
    • Select the General column or Invoice Report option.
    • Set the date range to cover the desired month.
    • View a summary of all invoices issued in that period, including GST details.
  4. Download or Export Invoices:
    • From the report view, use the Download or Export button to save invoices as PDF files.
    • You can download individual invoices or export a batch report for submission.
  5. Check Compliance:
    • Ensure the downloaded invoices meet government compliance standards.
    • Each invoice should include: invoice number, date, buyer/seller details, GSTIN, HSN/SAC codes, itemized taxes, and total amount.

Tips

  • Always double-check your GSTIN and company details before generating invoices.
  • Use the Preview option to verify invoice format before downloading.
  • Keep digital copies of all invoices for your records and compliance.
