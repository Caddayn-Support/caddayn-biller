How to Manage the Same Product Under Different Brands in Caddayn Biller

In Caddayn Biller, you may have the same product (e.g., Sugar) available from multiple brands (e.g., Brand A, Brand B). Properly managing brands helps you track sales, purchases, and inventory for each brand separately, ensuring accurate reporting and stock management.

Step-by-Step Guide: Add a Brand

  1. Go to the Master module from the main menu.
  2. Select the Create submodule.
  3. Click on Brand.
  4. Enter the brand name (e.g., "Brand A") and any relevant details.
  5. Click Save to add the new brand.
üí° Tip: Add all brands you deal with before adding products to ensure smooth product entry.

How to Attach a Brand to a Product

  1. Go to Items under Master Data.
  2. Select the product you want to assign a brand to.
  3. Edit the product details and choose the appropriate brand from the dropdown.
  4. Save the changes.
ÔøΩ Example: If you sell "Sugar" from both "Brand A" and "Brand B", create two products: "Sugar - Brand A" and "Sugar - Brand B". This allows you to track inventory and sales for each brand separately.
