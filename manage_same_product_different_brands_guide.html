Managing the Same Product Under Different Brands in Caddayn Biller

In Caddayn Biller, you may have the same product (e.g., "Sugar") available from multiple brands (e.g., "Brand A", "Brand B"). Properly managing brands helps you track sales, purchases, and inventory for each brand separately, ensuring accurate reporting and stock management.

Why Manage Products by Brand?

  • Accurate Inventory: Track stock levels for each brand individually.
  • Better Analytics: Analyze sales and purchases by brand.
  • Easy Reordering: Identify which brands are selling faster or need restocking.
  • Clear Product Listings: Avoid confusion between similar products from different brands.

How to Add a Brand in Caddayn Biller

  1. Go to the Master module from the main menu.
  2. Select the Create submodule.
  3. Click on Brand.
  4. Enter the brand name (e.g., "Brand A") and any relevant details.
  5. Click Save to add the new brand.

How to Add the Same Product Under Different Brands

  1. Go to Items under Master Data.
  2. Click Add Product.
  3. Enter the product name (e.g., "Sugar").
  4. Select the appropriate Brand from the dropdown (e.g., "Brand A").
  5. Fill in other product details (e.g., size, price, barcode).
  6. Click Save.
  7. Repeat the process for the same product with a different brand (e.g., "Sugar" with "Brand B").
üìù Note: Each brand-product combination is treated as a separate item for tracking and reporting.

Tracking and Reporting by Brand

  1. Go to the Reports or Analytics section.
  2. Filter or group your sales, purchases, and inventory data by Brand.
  3. Review which brands are performing best and make informed business decisions.

Customer Care Assistant Checklist

  • Guide owners to add all relevant brands before adding products.
  • Ensure each product is linked to the correct brand during entry.
  • Assist in using brand-based filters in reports for better insights.
  • Help owners update or add new brands as their inventory changes.
