What is the Use of Category? How is it Helpful for an Owner to Track?

Category in Caddayn Biller is used to check which products belong to which group. Using categories, owners can group products and view analytics on sales and purchases by category. Categories can be easily created from the Master module within the Create submodule.

Step-by-Step Guide: Create a Category

  1. Go to the Master module from the main menu.
  2. Select the Create submodule.
  3. Click on Category.
  4. Enter the category name and any relevant details.
  5. Click Save to add the new category.
ðŸ’¡ Tip: Use clear and descriptive names for categories (e.g., "Beverages", "Electronics").

How to Assign Products to a Category

  1. Go to Items under Master Data.
  2. Select the product you want to assign.
  3. Edit the product details and choose the appropriate category from the dropdown.
  4. Save the changes.

How to Track and Analyze by Category

  1. Go to the Reports or Analytics section.
  2. Filter or group your sales and purchase data by category.
  3. Review which categories are performing best and identify trends.
ðŸ“Š Note: Assigning all products to categories ensures accurate analytics and reporting.
