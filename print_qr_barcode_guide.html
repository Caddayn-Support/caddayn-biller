How to Print QR Codes or Barcodes in Caddayn Biller

Caddayn Biller allows you to generate and print QR codes or barcodes for your products, making inventory and sales management easier. This guide explains the steps and methods to print QR codes or barcodes directly from the application using a sticker printer.

Step 1: Add a Purchase Entry

  1. Go to the Purchase module in your Caddayn Biller dashboard.
  2. Create a new purchase entry by adding the products and specifying the quantity purchased.
  3. Save the purchase entry.
üõí Note: The quantity you enter in the purchase will determine how many QR/barcode stickers you can print.

Step 2: Print QR Codes or Barcodes

  1. After saving the purchase entry, look for the Print QR/Barcode option (usually available in the purchase details or actions).
  2. Click the Print QR/Barcode button.
  3. A print preview window will open, showing the QR codes or barcodes for each item, matching the quantity you entered.
  4. Click Print to send the codes to your connected sticker printer.
üñ®Ô∏è Printer: Use a compatible sticker/label printer for best results. Make sure your printer settings match the label size.

Other Methods to Print Codes

  1. From Product List:
    • Go to Items under Master Data.
    • Find the product and click the Print QR/Barcode icon.
  2. From Product Details:
    • Open the product details page and use the Print button next to the code image.
  3. Using Label Templates:
    • Navigate to Barcode/QR Templates (if available).
    • Select or customize a template, choose products, and print the labels.

Troubleshooting

  • If the QR or barcode is not printing correctly, check your printer settings and ensure the correct paper/label size is selected.
  • Make sure your browser allows pop-ups for print preview windows.
  • For faded or unclear prints, try adjusting your printer quality settings.
