How to Print QR Codes or Barcodes in Caddayn Biller

Step 1: Enter Purchase or Add Product

To print a QR code or barcode, first ensure the product is entered in your inventory. You can do this by adding a purchase entry or creating a new product in the Items section.

Step 2: Go to Barcode/QR Printing

  1. Navigate to the Barcode/QR Printing option in the menu.
  2. Select the product(s) for which you want to print codes.
  3. Choose the type (QR or Barcode) and the label size.
  4. Click Print to generate the codes.

Step 3: Use a Compatible Printer

For best results, use a sticker printer compatible with your system. Most USB sticker printers work with Caddayn Biller. Make sure the printer is installed and selected as the default printer.
